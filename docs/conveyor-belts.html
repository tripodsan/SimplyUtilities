Conveyor Belts

Overview

Conveyor Belts are constructs that when stepped on, cause the player to be moved about. Conveyor Belts are constructed by placing a block of magenta glazed terracotta, one block of wool of any color, and a concrete block of a color listed below.
The wool and the concrete are interchangeable, but the terracotta must be at the bottom.

Behaviour

The rotation of the terracotta determines what direction the belt moves in, and the color of the concrete effects the speed:

  • Red: 1
  • Orange: 2
  • Yellow: 3
  • Lime: 4
  • Blue: 5
